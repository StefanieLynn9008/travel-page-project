Why London?

London is Calling

A visit to London is a must for everyone as this is one city that will make you fall in love with it so much that you'll want to keep coming back. With stunning architecture, gorgeous gardens, magnificent towers, busy streets, many shops, and some iconic sites, London is everything dreamy.

Hop on a double-decker bus and enjoy the sites of the city, travel in the underground metro, spot the iconic black taxi, and pose for an amazing photo inside the red telephone booth. Indisputably, a visit to London will bring everything you have read about or seen on screen to life in an opulent and classy manner.

Steeped in history, London has some of the most famous museums, iconic cricket stadiums, parks and gardens, palaces, and bridges. One of the oldest cities in the world, London is extremely modern while preserving its values. London's art scene; the spectacular and grand productions of theatre like Wicked, The Lion King, and The Cursed Child; exhilarating nightlife; and the food, make the city simply the best.

My Favorite Cafes in London

Intermission Coffee

Intermission Coffee in West Hampstead is a cozy haven known for expertly crafted brews and a vibrant arts scene. With exceptional coffee and pastries, it's the perfect spot for a delightful pause in the heart of West Hampstead's creative energy.

Address

Unit 2, Hardy Building West Hampstead

What I like about it

It is a great little refuge from the rhythm of the day to recharge your mind and body.

Paradox Design + Coffee

Paradox Design + Coffee in Netil Market, London Fields, is a chic hub for coffee and creativity. With expertly brewed coffee and a stylish ambiance, it's the perfect spot for design enthusiasts and coffee lovers. The minimalist setting showcases local designers' works, offering a unique blend of taste and aesthetics in the heart of London Fields.

Address

13 - 23 Westgate street Unit A Netil Market, Hackney

What I like about it

It continues to be a place that is a catalyst for more wonderful inspiring connections, conversations and the plethora of other lovely magical things.

Origin Coffee

Origin Coffee on Charlotte Road, near the British Library, is a coffee gem renowned for its top-notch brews and modern ambiance. With a commitment to quality, it's a perfect spot for a meticulously crafted cup of coffee in the heart of London.

Address

65 Charlotte Rd

What I like about it

It is a fun place in East London where cuppings, events, and exceptional coffee bring people together.
